"Turning Scars to Stars is a non-profit organization dedicated to healing, empowerment, and transformation. We help survivors of trauma—especially women and youth—reclaim their lives through counseling, education, and community support."

What we Stand For?

Our Mission

Turning Scars to Stars Foundation is committed to providing humanitarian services to inner-city teenagers, particularly those who are vulnerable to abuse, drugs, and violence. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and empower these young individuals, helping them turn their lives around.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every teenager, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to grow in a safe, supportive environment, free from the threats of abuse and violence.

Our Purpose

Turning Scars to Stars Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organization business located in the Ogun State which would be involved in the provision of humanitarian services to inner city teenagers, especially those most vulnerable to abuses, drugs and violence.

OUR STORY

Born from personal experience and driven by compassion, Turning Scars to Stars was founded to bring healing to those who have suffered trauma, addiction, and social rejection. What began as a support circle for survivors is now a global movement of transformation.

  • 2018: The Seed is Planted

    After overcoming her own struggles, our founder began community outreach sessions in across various regions in the South-West.

  • 2020: The Registration

    We officially registered as an NGO and launched our first rehabilitation program for women in distress.

  • 2023: 5,000 Lives Reached

    With a team of counselors, volunteers, and partners, we expanded into multiple states and reached thousands with our awareness campaigns.
